India: General Introduction

Key Facts

  • India is the 7th largest country by area and 1st largest by population.
  • It occupies 2.4% of the total area of the lithosphere.
  • Total population is 17.5% as per 2011 census.
  • Land boundary: ~15,200 km; Coastline boundary: ~7,516.6 km.
  • Population density: 382 per sq. km.

Extreme Points

  • Southernmost: Mainland (Kanyakumari); Country (Indira Point/Pygmalion Point).
  • Northernmost: Indira Col (Near Siachen Glacier, J&K).
  • Westernmost: Guhar Moti (Gujarat) | Easternmost: Kibithu (AP).

Neighbouring Countries & Boundaries

  • Shortest boundary: Afghanistan (106 km).
  • Largest boundary: Bangladesh (4096.7 km).
  • Radcliffe line (Pakistan), Durand line (Afghanistan), McMahon line (China), Palk Strait (Sri Lanka).

States & Territories

  • Largest state (Area): Rajasthan | (Population): UP.
  • Smallest state (Area): Goa | (Population): Sikkim.
  • Union Territories: Largest (Area: J&K, Pop: Delhi) | Smallest (Area/Pop: Lakshadweep).
