India: General Introduction

General Stats & Population

  • India is the 7th largest country by area and 1st largest by population.
  • Occupies 2.4% of the total area of the lithosphere.
  • Population percentage: 16.7% (2001 Census) and 17.5% (2011 Census).
  • Land boundary: ~15,200 km | Coastline boundary: ~7,516.6 km.
  • Population Density: 382 per sq. km.

Extreme Points

  • Southernmost Point (Mainland): Kanyakumari (TN).
  • Southernmost Point (Country): Indira Point or Pygmalion Point.
  • Northernmost Point: Indira Col, Near Siachen Glacier (J&K).
  • Westernmost Point: Guhar Moti (Gujarat) | Easternmost Point: Kibithu (AP).

Neighbouring Countries & Boundary Lines

  • Shortest boundary: Afghanistan (106 km).
  • Longest boundary: Bangladesh (4096.7 km).
  • Boundary Lines: Pakistan (Radcliffe line), Afghanistan (Durand line), China (McMahon line), Sri Lanka (Palk strait).

States Bordering International Countries

  • Pakistan: Ladakh, J&K, Punjab, Rajasthan, Gujarat.
  • Afghanistan: Ladakh.
  • China: Ladakh, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, Sikkim, Arunachal Pradesh.
  • Nepal: Uttarakhand, UP, Bihar, West Bengal, Sikkim.
  • Bhutan: Sikkim, West Bengal, Assam, Arunachal Pradesh.
  • Myanmar: Arunachal Pradesh, Nagaland, Manipur, Mizoram.
  • Sri Lanka: Coastline boundary with Tamil Nadu.

Area, Population & Territories

  • Largest State: Area (Rajasthan), Population (UP).
  • Smallest State: Area (Goa), Population (Sikkim).
  • Union Territories: Largest Area (J&K), Largest Population (Delhi) | Smallest Area & Population (Lakshadweep).
  • Coastline: Longest coastline is Gujarat.
