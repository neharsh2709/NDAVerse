India: General Introduction

Key Facts

  • India is the 7th largest country by area and 1st largest by population.
  • Occupies 2.4% of the total area of the lithosphere.
  • Population: 17.5% as per 2011 census.
  • Land boundary: ~15,200 km; Coastline: ~7,516.6 km.
  • Population density: 382 per sq. km.

Extreme Points

  • Southernmost: Mainland (Kanyakumari); Country (Indira Point).
  • Northernmost: Indira Col (J&K).
  • Westernmost: Guhar Moti (Gujarat) | Easternmost: Kibithu (AP).

Neighbouring Countries

  • Shortest boundary: Afghanistan (106 km).
  • Largest boundary: Bangladesh (4096.7 km).
  • Major lines: Radcliffe (Pakistan), Durand (Afghanistan), McMahon (China), Palk Strait (Sri Lanka).
