India: General Introduction

Key Facts

  • India is the 7th largest country by area.
  • India is the 1st largest country by population.
  • It occupies 2.4% of the total area of the lithosphere.
  • Total population percentage: 16.7% (2001) and 17.5% (2011 Census).
  • Land boundary is about 15,200 km; coastline boundary is about 7,516.6 km.
  • Population density is 382 per sq. km.

Extreme Points of India

  • Southernmost Point (Mainland): Kanyakumari, TN
  • Southernmost Point (Country): Indira Point or Pygmalion Point
  • Northernmost Point: Indira Col, Near Siachen Glacier (J&K)
  • Westernmost Point: Guhar Moti (Gujarat)
  • Easternmost Point: Kibithu (AP)
